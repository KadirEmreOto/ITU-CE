Single Player

The game is set during World War II and the campaign mode is experienced through the perspectives of four soldiers: one in the Red Army, one in the United States Army, and two in the British Army. It contains four individual campaigns, split into three stories, with a total of twenty-seven missions. Activision officially announced the game on April 7, 2005 in a press release. Many features were added and changed from the original Call of Duty. The most notable change is the regenerating health. Additions include an icon that indicates a nearby grenade about to explode. (More Info)

  • Russian Campaign: First, you are a soldier who works for Red Army and you are defending Moscow from Germans. Also first chapter is the training chapter. After that Germans are attacking Stalingrad and you are defending again. These Soviet Chapters in 1942 will strain you, because Germans have tactical advantages. However, in 1943, you will be relax, because you will be the advantageous side. In addition, your character in these chapters represents very well-known people: Vasily Zaysev.
  • British Campaign: You are playing role of John Davis who is fighting with Germans in North Africa. You are saving some cities and towns in Egypt from Germans and battling as a tank commander, David Welsh. After, you will have new missions in Toujane and East France.
  • American Campaign: The american missions start with the longest chapter in the game. This chapter, which is about The Normandy landings, is one of the most realistic and difficult chapters. You are playing role of Bill Taylor, who is trying to save Normandy. In next chapter, you are suppressing the last resistance of Germans in Bergstein. Besides, the last mission is Operation Plunder. You are attacking a city in German and winning the war. Thus the game is ending.

Multiplayer

The game can be played in multiplayer mode on game servers which can hold a maximum of 64 players. The Soldiers of the four countries (British, American, German, Russian) are fighting with each other. The game features four game types following below:

  • DM (Deathmatch): In this mode, there is no team and there is no friend. Everbody is hostile to you, and you must kill whoever you see.
  • TDM (Team Deathmatch): In this mode, there are two teams and teams try to kill each other. The team which have more scores at the end of game or reach the score, determined before starting game, wins the game.
  • SD (Search and Destroy):There are two teams in that mode. One team which is allies (British, Russian, American) tries to place bomb in vehicle that appear as star on the map, other team which is axis (German) tries to save the vehicle without dying.
  • CTF (Capture The Flag): The purpose in that mode is to take the flag belong to aganist team and bring it your own region, but your flag must not be taken by aganist team. Every flag that captured gives 10 point to player who capture the flag and gives 1 point to team that capture the flag. The team which capture more flags at the end of game or reach the score, determined before starting game, wins the game.
